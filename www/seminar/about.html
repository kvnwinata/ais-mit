About PERMIAS-MA Lecture/Seminar Series

Two things: to help foster an effective scholarly interactions among Indonesian students & scholars in the Greater Boston Area, and to cultivate a highly interdisciplinary mindset within the Indonesian intellectual community under PERMIAS in general. These are the main mission objectives that PERMIAS MA wants to achieve through its Scholar and Strategic Division.

This seminar series serves as an opportunity for Indonesian students/scholars etc to learn from one another about their respective experience and expertise. The seminars are held approximately once a month during the schoolyear, and they cover a relatively broad range of academic and/or practical disciplines.

If you would like to nominate your friends/colleagues (or volunteer yourself) to be a seminar speaker, you can do so by filling out this online form.
